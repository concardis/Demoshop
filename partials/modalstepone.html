Step 1/4: Checklist and Configuration


You can initialise the modal in just a few steps. Only your Merchant ID and your API-Key are needed to continue.
The merchant id and the Api-Key can be found in the Payengine Merchant Center under "My Account".
To perform a request a base64 encoded version of "MerchantID:APIKey" is needed. Use your own generator, or change the fields below and use the "generate" button.


# Field Value
1 Merchant ID
2 Api-Key
3 base64
